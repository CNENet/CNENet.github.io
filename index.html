Clinical Nurse Educator Network UK (CNENet)

The Clinical Nurse Educator Network UK (CNENet) is a professional network for clinical nurse educators working across the United Kingdom.

CNENet UK creates collaborative spaces for educators to share best practice, support professional development, and strengthen the quality of clinical education within healthcare settings. We maintain several social media groups (see links below). We also provide a newsletter, regular events such as webinars and our yearly conference.
