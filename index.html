Clinical Nurse Educator Network UK (CNENet)

The Clinical Nurse Educator Network UK (CNENet) is a professional network for clinical nurse educators working across the United Kingdom.

CNENet provides a collaborative space for educators to share best practice, support professional development, and strengthen the quality of clinical education within healthcare settings.

The network is open to nurses involved in clinical education in practice, education, and leadership roles.
