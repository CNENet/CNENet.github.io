Clinical Nurse Educator Network UK (CNENet)

The Clinical Nurse Educator Network UK (CNENet) is a volunteer-run professional network for all clinical nurse educators working across the United Kingdom.

CNENet provides a collaborative space for educators to share best practice, support professional development, and strengthen the quality of clinical education within healthcare settings.

The network is open to all clinical educators who are registered nurses working in the United Kingdom.
